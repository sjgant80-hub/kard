A turn-based card duel · 7 factions · 56 cards · audit-chained matches.

Build a 30-card deck across MAGNA · LIBERTY · CROWN · EQUITY · HEARTH · GUILD · ADMIRALTY. Duel an AI opponent. Every move is hash-chained. Winner gets a signed κ-trophy. Works offline. No accounts. No cloud.
